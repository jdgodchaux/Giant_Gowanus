Meet Giant Gowanus

Available everywhere music is digitally sold or streamed, including these fine purveyors:

Giant Gowanus is:

Holly Orr (guitar, keyboard, bass and vocals)
Nancy Nicholson (bass, guitar and vocals)
JD Godchaux (drums and vocals); jdgodchaux 'at' gmail 'dot' com

Giant Gowanus derives their musical power from the banks of the highly toxic canal that bears their name. Sometimes heavy, sometimes quiet, GG always brings it – it being the rock – with Holly Orr and Nancy Nicholson melding their gorgeous voices while playing some sick guitar and bass lines and JD Godchaux bringing the percussive thunder. You will love Giant Gowanus!
